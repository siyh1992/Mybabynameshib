15 Meaningful Baby Names and Their Origins

Looking for names rich with meaning? These 15 baby names each hold cultural or symbolic significance, perfect for parents seeking something thoughtful and heartfelt.

  1. Amara – Girl – Igbo (African): "Grace, Mercy"
  2. Theo – Boy – Greek: "Gift of God"
  3. Alina – Girl – Slavic: "Bright, Beautiful"
  4. Ezra – Boy – Hebrew: "Helper"
  5. Nia – Girl – Swahili: "Purpose"
  6. Liam – Boy – Irish: "Strong-willed warrior"
  7. Sarai – Girl – Hebrew: "Princess"
  8. Milan – Unisex – Slavic: "Gracious, Dear"
  9. Elio – Boy – Italian/Spanish: "Sun"
  10. Anaya – Girl – Sanskrit: "Care, Protection"
  11. Kai – Unisex – Hawaiian: "Sea"
  12. Asha – Girl – Sanskrit: "Hope, Life"
  13. Zion – Boy – Hebrew: "Highest point"
  14. Calliope – Girl – Greek: "Beautiful voice"
  15. Ravi – Boy – Sanskrit: "Sun"
